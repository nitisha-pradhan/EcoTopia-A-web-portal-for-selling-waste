About Us

"Never doubt that a small group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world; indeed, it is the only thing that ever has." - Margaret Mead

We, at Uirentia help the common people to recycle their plastic, paper, metal, glass and e-waste with the comfort of being at home. Our aim is to make recycling fun and easily available for all.

We first started this website as a college project in the third year, and the idea took off. We've always had the passion to work towards helping the society and saving the environment. Starting with just 3 members, we are now a team of 59 individuals, and are affiliated with many major companies such as Amazon and Flipkart. We plan on expanding our company's reach to other cities as well, and hope to do it soon!
